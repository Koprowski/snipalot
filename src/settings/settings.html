Click a binding, then press the desired keys. Esc cancels, Backspace clears the field. Combos must include a modifier (Ctrl, Shift, or Alt) plus one regular key. Conflicts with another app may show a notification at registration time.

sec

After the region is selected (or immediately in fullscreen mode), a 3-2-1 countdown gives you time to switch focus before the recording starts. Esc cancels. Default 3. Set 0 for instant; type a custom number above 10 if you want a longer pre-roll.

About

Snipalot records your screen with live numbered annotations, transcribes your voice with Whisper (a local speech model that runs entirely on your CPU — no API key, no internet), and puts an LLM-ready prompt on your clipboard. Snipalot itself never contacts any cloud service; what you do with the prompt is up to you.
